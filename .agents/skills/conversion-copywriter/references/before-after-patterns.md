# Before and After Patterns

Use these patterns when the user says the copy feels weak, generic, flat, too long, or not persuasive enough.

## 1. Generic headline -> concrete offer
Before:
- We help businesses grow with digital innovation.

After:
- Short-form videos, websites, and AI workflows that help modern brands sell with less friction.

Why it works:
- names the offer
- narrows the audience context
- replaces abstraction with concrete categories

## 2. Feature list -> benefit-led service description
Before:
- We provide strategy, automation, design, and implementation.

After:
- We design the site, content, and automation flow together so your brand looks sharper and wastes less time chasing manual work.

Why it works:
- turns feature categories into one meaningful outcome
- gives the reader a reason to care

## 3. Weak CTA -> action CTA
Before:
- Learn more

After:
- See how it would work for your brand

Why it works:
- reduces ambiguity
- tells the reader what happens next

## 4. Puffy testimonial -> grounded testimonial
Before:
- Amazing service, super professional, highly recommended.

After:
- They tightened our message, delivered fast, and made the site easier for clients to understand.

Why it works:
- sounds more believable
- highlights concrete value instead of generic praise

## 5. Vague case-study blurb -> transformation story
Before:
- We helped the client improve their online presence.

After:
- The original site looked credible enough to exist, but not clear enough to convert. We rebuilt the message around one offer, simplified the flow, and gave prospects a clearer next step.

Why it works:
- shows the before state
- explains the bridge
- makes the improvement legible without inventing metrics

## 6. Bland hook -> tension hook
Before:
- Here are some tips for improving your landing page.

After:
- If people land on your page and still cannot tell what you sell, the problem is usually the copy before the design.

Why it works:
- introduces tension
- states a specific problem
- earns the next sentence
